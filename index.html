Features

      

        

          
Always alive

          
Not a static wallpaper. Orbit breathes with your activity — now playing, live stats, ambient motion when idle. Your screen earns its place.

        

        

          
Your stack, your rules

          
Point Orbit at Tautulli, Home Assistant, or SignalRGB. Local APIs only — no accounts, no cloud, no phone-home. You control the data.

        

        

          
Any display

          
Built for the Corsair Xeneon 14″. Runs on any USB‑C secondary monitor. Auto‑detects non‑primary screens so setup is one click.

        

        

          
Touch‑ready

          
Designed for touch panels. Tap to switch effects, scrub brightness, or see what's queued next — fast, low‑latency, and satisfying.
