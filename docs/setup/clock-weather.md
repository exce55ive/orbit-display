# Clock & Weather — Time and Forecast Panel

The Clock & Weather panel shows the current time, date, and a 5-day weather forecast — no accounts or API keys needed.

## What you'll need

- An internet connection (for weather data)
- That's it — no accounts, no API keys, no registration

## What it shows

- Current time and date (from your Windows system clock)
- 5-day weather forecast including:
  - Feels-like temperature
  - Humidity
  - UV index
  - Wind speed and direction

## Step-by-step

### 1. Enter your city

1. Open Orbit and click the **⚙** button in the bottom bar to open Settings.
2. Go to **Settings → Clock & Weather**.
3. Type your **city name** in the location field.
4. Save — the forecast loads automatically.

That's it. Weather data comes from [Open-Meteo](https://open-meteo.com/), a free weather service that doesn't require any registration.

## Where to enter it in Orbit

**Settings → Clock & Weather**

- **Location** — Your city name (e.g. "Perth" or "London")

## Troubleshooting

**Wrong location or no forecast showing:**
- Double-check the spelling of your city name.
- If your town isn't recognised, try a nearby major city instead.
- Make sure the machine running Orbit has internet access — the weather data comes from an external service.

**Clock shows the wrong time:**
- Orbit uses your Windows system clock. If the time is wrong, check your system time and timezone settings in Windows.
